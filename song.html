My song

I see the world through a midnight window, 

Shadows dancing in a silent show. 

The city's breathing, soft and low, 

And I don’t know where I’ll go.

 

Footsteps echo down the empty street, 

Dreams and doubts where strangers meet. 

A coffee cup, a cigarette glow, 

Another hour, another row.

 

But I keep walking, heart in hand, 

No map, no plan, no promised land. 

Just chasing echoes, chasing light, 

In the long hum of the night.
